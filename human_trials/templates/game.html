      How to Play
      Crewmates
      • Complete all your assigned tasks, or
      • Vote out all Impostors in meetings
      Impostors
      • Kill Crewmates until they are outnumbered, or
      • Survive until the time limit runs out (Turn Count > 10)
      On Each Turn (90s)
      • Move: Travel to an adjacent room
      • Do Task: Complete a task in your room (Crewmates)
      • Report: Report a dead body to call a meeting
      • Emergency Meeting: Call a meeting from the Cafeteria
      • Kill: Eliminate a nearby Crewmate (Impostors only)
      • Vent: Teleport between vent-connected rooms (Impostors only)
      Emergency Meetings
      • Discuss who you think the Impostor is
      • Each player speaks once per discussion round
      • Then everyone votes. Player with most votes gets ejected
      • Ties result in no ejection
      • Dead players must observe (pass turn) but cannot vote or speak
      Tips
      • You can only see players who are in the same room as you
      • Watch the game log carefully. It records when players leave your room
      • Be careful using Impostor actions like killing and venting - Witnesses in the same room will see it and reveal you
      • If you go fail to submit an action on time, a null action is submitted automatically
